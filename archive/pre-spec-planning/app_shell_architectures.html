Universal App Shells

A conceptual guide to how modern applications are structured, from broad categories down to the underlying boilerplate architecture.

1. The Application Payload (Categories)

No matter what technology you use, the "top layer" of your app falls into a category based on user intent. A universal shell is designed to be completely agnostic to these categories, allowing you to build any of the following inside it:

  • Productivity & Workflow: Note-taking, spreadsheets, task managers.
  • Games & Entertainment: Interactive media, casual puzzles.
  • Utilities: Calculators, file managers, local tools.
  • Social & Communication: Chat apps, forums.
  • E-Commerce & Lifestyle: Shopping, habit trackers.
